    Welcome to my portfolio !

    I am a software developer with a music background. I am located in Boston, Massachusetts. I'm interested in developing web pages, games, and DAWs (digital audio workplaces). The DAWs I've used in the past to produce music are great, but I feel like there could be a more user-friendly version so that more people can explore music. What sets me apart is my team working skills, I can get along with just about anybody. One of my strengths is that I'm very capable of communicating with my teammates which creates an efficient and friendly work enviornment.

    About Luan

    After I was finished with school I started producing music, and bartending. I made a lot of connections through my work and realized that I could develop websites to give the artists I've met an edge while building my development portfolio.

    I’m very passionate about becoming a great web developer, learning these new languages allows me to create art in a completely different way than I ever have before. Gaining this new knowledge allows me see the world through a different lens as well. I look forward to learning from the developers that I work with in the future!
