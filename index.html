Intro

Welcome to my portfolio !

I am a software developer with a music background. I'm originally from Boston, Massachusetts. I'm interested in developing web pages, games, and DAWs (digital audio workplaces). The DAWs I've used in the past to produce music were great, but I feel like there could be a more user-friendly version so that more people can explore music. One thing that sets me apart is my team working skills, I can get along with just about anybody. One of my strengths is that I'm very capable of communicating with my team. Communication creates an efficient and friendly work environment.
